About me

My name is Alek Stošić, born in 1966.

In my seemingly never-ending quest (or constant search) for my own self and a job that would help people and move them towards new and positive energy, I finally stopped at the Divine Therapy – Chiropractic.

I have found my inspiration in meditation, which is the only precondition for finding balance between material and immaterial worlds, as well as an escape from all the stress that weighs in on modern/contemporary man.

On my web site I have shared with you everything that chiropractic truly represents: a problem that we overcome – healing.

My previous profession was about aviation and aero-technology. Between two passions I chose to help human beings.

My current wish is to make something completely alien and unknown to human consciences closer to all of you, to help you find yourself through that most tenuous of things between the spiritual (meditation) and material – chiropractic.

In hopes of you finding your own self, Alek Stošić
